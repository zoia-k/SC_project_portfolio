Interactive landing

I can tell you about 3 big pluses of interactive landing page:

  3. Landing page encourages users to take a specific action - sign up for a free trial period, book a service, or make a purchase.

Because landing pages are so targeted, they are extremely effective at driving conversions and lowering lead generation and sales costs.
